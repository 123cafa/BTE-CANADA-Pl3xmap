Iqaluit, NU

Iqaluit is the capital and only city in Nunavut. It is the northermost city in Canada and enjoys Aurora Borealis year round. Mostly inhabited by Inuits, many buildings incorporate Inuit art designs. St. Jude's Anglican Cathedral is shaped like an igloo, its altar shaped like a sled and the cross is composed of two crossed narwhal tusks.
