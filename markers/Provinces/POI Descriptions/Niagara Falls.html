Niagara Falls, ON

Niagara Falls is composed of three waterfalls on the border of Ontario and New York. These falls have the highest flow rate of any waterfall in North America. They are also known for the terrifying practice of barrel jumping, tumbling over the falls while inside a barrel, with mixed results.
