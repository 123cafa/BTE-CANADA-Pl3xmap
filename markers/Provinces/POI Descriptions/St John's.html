St John's, NL

St John's is the capital of Newfoundland and Labrador. Sitting on the easternmost point of Canada, it was a common landing spot for ships traversing the ocean. This led to a tumultuous history as nations waged war to claim it. Downtown is mainly composed of historic fishermen homes, sheds and storage turned into housing. It earned the name Jelly Bean Row for its colorful tightly packed homes.
